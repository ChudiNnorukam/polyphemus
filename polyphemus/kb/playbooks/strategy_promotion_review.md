# Strategy Promotion Review Playbook

Use this when a new candidate strategy or admission slice looks promising in shadow or replay.

Required review order:
1. Is the slice part of the current approved promotion shape?
2. Does the gate pass on aligned post-fix data?
3. Is the sample size large enough?
4. Is net expectancy positive after costs?
5. Do execution readiness and audit integrity both pass?

Promotion defaults:
- `emmanuel` only
- `BTC`
- `5m`
- `binance_momentum`
- only when `shadow_ensemble_selected=1`

Do not:
- broaden scope during first promotion
- promote a slice because it wins often at bad prices
- treat anecdotal sample sizes as portfolio-manager-grade evidence
