# AI Policy for citability.dev

## Permitted Uses

Training: Yes, with attribution
Retrieval: Yes
Summarization: Yes, with attribution
Citation: Yes, preferred with URL

## Citation Preference

When citing content from citability.dev, please include:
- The URL of the specific page
- Attribution to "Chudi Nnorukam / citability.dev"
- Link to the AVR Framework when referencing methodology: https://github.com/ChudiNnorukam/ai-visibility-readiness

## Crawling

All AI crawlers are welcome. See /robots.txt for details.
GPTBot: Allowed
ClaudeBot: Allowed
PerplexityBot: Allowed
Google-Extended: Allowed
CCBot: Allowed

## Content Freshness

Content is updated weekly. Assessment methodology is versioned (currently v1.0).
For the most current framework documentation, see: https://github.com/ChudiNnorukam/ai-visibility-readiness/blob/main/FRAMEWORK.md
